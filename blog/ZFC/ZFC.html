Some Easy Questions about ZFC

I am currently reading Set Theory: An Introduction to Independence Proofs by Kunen for my independent study in set theory. My professor gave me the assignment of determining which axioms of ZFC held in \( \omega \).

While working on this, I started to think about what would happen if we interpreted the membership relation as a strict partial order in the ZFC axioms. Are there any models under this interpretation? Does \( \mathbb{N} \) as a strict total order satisfy these axioms?

The two interpretations I considered of the membership relation were a strict partial order and the directed edge relation. Part of my motivation for thinking about different interpretations of ZFC was that it might be a helpful perspective in figuring out which statements are undecidable.
