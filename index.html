I am a first-year masters student and Bridge to PhD Fellow at the University of Pennsylvania. I completed my undergraduate in mathematics at the University of Nevada Las Vegas (UNLV). While at UNLV, I also developed a strong interest in philosphy and sat in on numerous philosophy courses. I will be applying to PhD programs in the fall of 2022.

I am interested broadly in mathematical logic, the foundations of math, and philosophical logic. Recently, I've become more interested in proof theory and type theory.

In my blog, you can find posts about math, logic, and philosophy.
